Key Facts

NBA champion rosters earn $547,000 per player — the highest per-player playoff bonus of any major American team sport.

The NBA playoff pool distributes money across 5 tiers, from $38K for first-round exits to $547K for champions.

NBA playoff bonuses are separate from salaries — players continue to earn contract salary during postseason games on top of these bonuses.
