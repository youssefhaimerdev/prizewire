Key Facts

State athletic commissions publish disclosed purses only — real fighter earnings can be 3-5x higher when PPV points and bonuses are included.

The UFC issues four $50,000 bonuses at most events: two Performance of the Night and one Fight of the Night (two fighters).

The gap between UFC and boxing pay is vast — Canelo earns $50M+ per fight while UFC's top earners get $1-3M disclosed per event.
