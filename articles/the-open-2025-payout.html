Scheffler Completes Three-of-Four Majors in 2025

Scottie Scheffler shot four sub-70 rounds at Royal Portrush to win the 153rd Open Championship by four strokes — his fourth career major and first Claret Jug. The win made him just a US Open victory shy of the Career Grand Slam, having previously won the Masters (multiple times), PGA Championship 2025, and now The Open 2025. The $3.1 million pushed his 2025 season earnings past $19 million and his career PGA Tour earnings to just shy of $100 million.

The R&A held the 2025 purse at $17 million — flat versus 2024 — making The Open the smallest purse of the four majors. Despite that, the winner's share of $3.1 million is still exceptional, and the $38,900 minimum for players who made the cut ensured solid paydays down the field.
