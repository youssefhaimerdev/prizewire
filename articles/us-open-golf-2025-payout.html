US Open 2025 Prize Money Breakdown

The United States Golf Association continued its pattern of year-on-year purse increases, pushing the 2025 US Open prize fund to a record $21.5 million. This maintained the US Open's position as the largest prize pool among the four major championships, surpassing The Masters ($22.5M in 2026, though $20M in 2025), the PGA Championship ($19M in 2025), and The Open Championship ($17M in 2025).

Distribution follows the standard PGA TOUR structure: the winner receives 18% of the total purse — a fixed percentage that scales all positions proportionally as purses grow. Players who miss the 36-hole cut receive a standard consolation payment.

McIlroy Completes the Career Grand Slam

Rory McIlroy's 2025 US Open victory was one of the most celebrated wins in recent golf history. Having won the Masters in April 2025 to finally complete the Career Grand Slam, McIlroy arrived at the US Open as the reigning Masters champion. His victory in both events in 2025 earned him $3.6M (Masters) + $3.87M (US Open) = $7.47 million in major championship money in a single season — an extraordinary achievement both competitively and financially.
