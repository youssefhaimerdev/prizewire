Key Facts

The Players Championship ($25M) pays more than any Major, making TPC Sawgrass the highest-stakes regular-season venue in golf.

Winner's share is always 18% of total purse on the PGA Tour — a fixed ratio that scales every payout proportionally.

Players who miss the cut still receive $4,300 at most events — a base consolation that scales up at Majors.
