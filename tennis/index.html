Key Facts

The US Open is the richest Grand Slam at $65 million total — and has maintained equal prize money for men and women since 1973.

Wimbledon 2025 raised its total purse to £53.5 million, with singles champions earning £3 million each — an 11.1% increase.

The ATP Finals pays its winner $4.73M — more than a Wimbledon semi-finalist — making it one of tennis's highest-value events.
