Key Facts

💸
Ohtani's Deferred Deal

Ohtani earns $2M in cash salary because 97% of his $700M contract is deferred to 2034-2043. The Dodgers essentially have him on a discount now, paying $680M after he retires.

🗽
New York's $765M Bet

The Mets' signing of Soto with zero deferred money means they pay full price, full schedule. Combined with Lindor, Wheeler, and others, NYC has baseball's most expensive roster by far.

⚾
The Deferred Era

MLB's new reality: $700M+ contracts with massive deferrals that reduce present-day value. Understanding nominal vs. present value is now essential to understanding baseball finance.
