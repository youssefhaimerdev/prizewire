Key Facts

Boxing has no salary cap and no union-negotiated base pay. Every fight purse is privately negotiated between fighter, promoter, and broadcaster — making boxing the most financially opaque major sport.

Top fighters earn through base purse + PPV points — a percentage of pay-per-view buys above a threshold. Canelo's PPV revenue can exceed his base purse for mega-fights.

Canelo's $400 million deal with Riyadh Season, signed early 2025, guarantees him $80-150M per fight across 4-5 bouts regardless of PPV performance.
